# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors exploring Martyn Davies's work, primarily prospective employers, collaborators, and peers in tech. They arrive via LinkedIn, referral, or search, looking to quickly understand who Martyn is, what he does, and whether he's worth contacting. Their context is scanning, not reading, they want signal fast.

## Product Purpose

A long-term portfolio and reference surface. The site exists to make hiring decisions easier for people who are already interested. Success looks like a visitor finding the right page, understanding the work, and reaching out, or leaving with a clear sense of Martyn's taste and capabilities.

## Brand Personality

Simple. Informative. Spacious.

## Anti-references

- SaaS landing pages with massive hero sections, big-number metrics, and gradient accents
- AI-generated slop: generic illustrations, meaningless buzzword copy, identical card grids
- Over-designed portfolios that prioritize novelty over clarity
- Dense, breathless layouts that feel like a resume crammed into a browser

## Design Principles

1. **Space is content.** Generous whitespace isn't empty, it's the signal that this person knows what to leave out.
2. **Show, don't tell.** Work speaks louder than claims. Let the projects and writing carry the weight.
3. **One click to clarity.** Every page should answer its question within one screen, no scroll-hunting required.
4. **Restraint is confidence.** No decorative flourishes. Every element must justify its presence.
5. **Plain text is a feature.** The site should feel complete and intentional even with CSS disabled.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Sensible defaults: focus indicators, semantic HTML, sufficient color contrast
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Pure markdown versions of every page available for screen readers and text-mode browsers
